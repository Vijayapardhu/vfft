\---



\# 1. Product Overview



VFFT (Velangi Free Fire Tournament) is an IPL-inspired franchise-based esports platform designed specifically for Free Fire communities.



The platform enables:



\* Player registration

\* Franchise creation

\* Player auctions

\* Team formation

\* Match management

\* Automatic fixture generation

\* Team standings

\* Player leaderboards

\* Hall of Fame

\* Multi-season support



The goal is to provide a professional esports ecosystem while remaining simple and accessible for village gamers.



\---



\# 2. Vision

\* Team Leader

\---



\## IPL-Style Auction



Players enter the auction pool.



Teams bid with virtual coins.



Features:



\* Base Price

\* Live Bids

\* Highest Bid

\* Sold Price

\* Sold Team



\---



\## Squad System



Maximum Squad Size:



10 Players



Playing Squad:



4 Players



Bench Players:



6 Players



\---



\## Match-Day Lineups



Thirty minutes before the match:



Team Leader submits:



\* Playing Four

\* Captain

\* Vice-Captain



Admin approves lineups.



After approval:



Lineup becomes locked.
